About Me

Me, that's a molecular biologist who started working in the IT sector a few years ago. Right now I am trying to find what I like most and I just started with front-end development.
My passions are nature (hiking & gardening), good food (traditional Austrian dishes & trying foreign recipes), making myself cosy at home & decorating our flat, quizzing (competeing with my boyfriend or winning together) and travelling.
First of all: I love the North. Especially a small island in the German North Sea called Amrum. However, I am really eager to get to know new cultures all around the world. Next stop: Marocco.
